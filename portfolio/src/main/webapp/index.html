Hobbies & Interests

Soccer has been a constant presence in my life and I both play it and watch professional matches regularly. In college, I usually play intramural soccer and organize some pick up games throughout the semester. My preferred playing positions are either left winger or central attacking midfielder (CAM) since I tend to be more of an offensive minded player and I prefer to put in a final pass rather than finishing a play myself. As a soccer fan I support the national teams of Ecuador and Italy although as of late being a fan of these teams has been painful to say the least. At the club level I support AS Roma from the Italian league and I have a soft spot for Barcelona from the Spanish league. With these latter two teams being my favorite clubs it should come as no surprise that my favorite soccer players of all time are Francesco Totti and Lionel Messi and they have undoubtedly had an influence on how I myself play the game.

One of my more recent interests has been coffee and it has been fascinating to learn about all the ways coffee can be brewed and enjoyed and how seemingly small changes in the procedures one uses have a profound impacton the final cup. Currently, I am in the process of building a collection of brewing methods with my first two purchases being a moka pot and a french press. I hope to add various pour over methods, an espresso machine, and a quality burr grinder to my set up soon.

One of my favorite activities over the past few years has been playing pool. This interest came about because my dorm has quite an extensive pool set up and at first it was a great way to destress with friends after a long day of classes and problem sets but it slowly became a great interest of mine and I've spent a lot of time trying to become better at the game. Eight ball and Nine ball pool are my favorite games but I also enjoy playing cutthroat pretty often. I hope to start a pool club once I'm back on campus and begin holding pool tournaments and expand our dorm's stash of pool supplies.
